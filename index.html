Featured Campaign

Black Business First

America was built on Black labor. Now we build our own. The Black Business First campaign mobilizes communities to redirect spending, create economic corridors, and build an ecosystem where Black dollars circulate and compound within our own communities.

Campaign Goals

  • Redirect $100M in annual spending to Black-owned businesses
  • Register 5,000 new Black-owned businesses nationally
  • Establish 12 Black Business Districts in major cities
  • Create a national directory of 20,000+ verified Black businesses
